Why Ramalo?

Ramalo is an integrated logistics company seeking to be the solution to your distribution needs.

We increase your access to market by availing your product to South Africa's growing e-commerce market by giving you the opportunity to lease our services, inclusive of fully financed and insured vehicles and trained drivers.

The growing demand for e-commerce, strengthened by COVID-19 has created a greater need for our services. Our affordable yet competitive delivery fees make our services accessible to your growing business.

How it works

Ramalo has a medium sized fleet
readily available for your corporation.

Lease A-class vehicles, motorbikes and/or tuk-tuks for your
smaller distribution needs. Our platform is geared with a
plug-and-play easy to use app engineered with API integration,

Our three-sided business models allows us to earn commission through orders, while customers pay a small delivery fee and drivers earn via delivery fees paid by customers.
