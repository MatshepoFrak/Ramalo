Become a Ramalo Driver

Gain driver benefits

We provide market entry to the e-commerce sector. We offer training of licenced and unlicenced drivers. As a Ramalo driver, you have the opportunity to complete a driver/biking licence through a Ramalo programme. Drivers are required to complete a customer service training course. Upon course completion, drivers receive a Ramalo vehicle and smartphone.

Trusted, professional drivers

Our drivers go through a criminal background check to ensure that our clients' and their customers feel that their products are safely delivered and received. Ramalo drivers embody certain values and a code of conduct, which we are proud of are friendly, professional, punctual and always deliver promptly with a smile on their face.
